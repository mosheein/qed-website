Our Courses

Amsterdam Quantum Technologies Course
Location: Amsterdam
Dates: September 19-21, 2024
▼
Audience

Candidates: Experienced tech employees who want to learn about the Quantum Market. The market is growing, and quantum computers and sensors will play a major role in the future of computational solutions — disrupting industries like finance, material science, drug discovery, logistics, and mobility.

Course Breakdown
  • Day 1: Reminder on linear algebra in two dimensions, complex numbers. Basics of the Stern-Gerlach experiment. Quantum Mechanics axioms.
  • Day 2: Superposition, Measurement, Entanglement, Bell Theorem, Bloch sphere. Market overview: Investments, companies, technologies, policies.
  • Day 3: Core Technologies — Superconductors, Trapped Ions, Photons, Neutral Atoms, Quantum Annealing, Quantum Sensing. Basic Quantum gates. Decoherence. Quantum software, algorithms, error correction.
