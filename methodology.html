Our Methodology

Our Methodology

Designed for Everyone — No Quantum Background Required

We believe anyone can start learning quantum. Our courses are built from the ground up, starting with intuitive concepts and clear explanations — no prior physics or math knowledge needed.

In-Person. Personal. Human.

We focus on creating an engaging, supportive learning environment:

  • Small groups ensure every participant receives personal attention
  • We use interactive sessions, real-world analogies, and time for reflection
  • We make sure no one is left behind

Led by a Trusted, Experienced Instructor

Joab Rosenberg has over a decade of experience teaching physics and philosophy in universities across Israel and Europe. He's trained hundreds of tech professionals — many with no prior background — to feel confident navigating the quantum world.

More Than Science — Real Market Insight

With deep roots in the quantum industry, Joab brings more than just academic knowledge. His understanding of the real-world landscape — from startups to global research efforts — helps participants see how quantum technologies are evolving and where the most exciting opportunities lie.
